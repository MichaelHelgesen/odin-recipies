Moussaka

Description

This lasagna is surely one of the worlds most delicious variants. Expect to get a new favourite!

It`s not difficult to make. The secret is in the ingrediences.

Ingrediences

  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Pizza bun
  • Ham
  • Bacon
  • Totato sauce

Steps

  1. Chop the ingrediences
  2. Fry the meat
  3. Mix it all together
  4. Bake in oven
  5. Enjoy!
